Mango Pudding

Description

After taking the time to make the main course, why not take the pressure off and enjoy this simple but delicious mango pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 large ripe mango
  • 6 scoops vanilla ice-cream
  • 2 tbsp thick cream
  • 3 passion fruits

Directions

  1. Peel and dice a large ripe mango; whizz to a purée in a food processor.
  2. Add 6 scoops of vanilla ice cream and 2 tbsp thick cream and whizz again.
  3. Spoon into bowls and scoop ½ passion fruit over each.
